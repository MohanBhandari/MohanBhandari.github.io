About Me

I am a PhD Scholar at Sirindhorn International Institute of Technology, Thammasat University, Thailand, specializing in Medical Image Processing. My research focuses on Multi-Scale Artificial Life for Segmentation of Medical Images.

As an Artificial Intelligence enthusiast and machine learning developer, I have sound knowledge of machine learning algorithms, probability, and explainable AI. I have collaborated with researchers from Harvard University, University of Tennessee, University of Texas, Ulster University, and other institutions worldwide.

I have several years of experience lecturing in programming-related subjects like AI, ML, image processing, data mining, data science, data structure, C, C++, and DBMS at various colleges in Nepal.
