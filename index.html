Profile Summary

Mohan Bhandari received Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Computer Engineering from Pokhara University and is currently pursuing a PhD in Medical Image Processing at Sirindhorn International Institute of Technology, Thammasat University, Thailand.

His research focuses on explainable AI in medical imaging, neural architecture search, vision-language models, and human blastocyst analysis. He is also active in editorial work, peer review, academic teaching, and international research collaboration.

He serves as an Associate Editor for Frontiers in Digital Health and has received multiple recognitions including the Excellent Foreign Scholarship at SIIT and a Best Research Paper Award.
