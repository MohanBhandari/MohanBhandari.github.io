Profile Summary

Mohan Bhandari received a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and a Master's degree in Computer Engineering from Pokhara University. He is currently a PhD Scholar specializing in Medical Image Processing at Sirindhorn International Institute of Technology (SIIT), Thammasat University, Thailand.

His research field includes explainable AI in medical imaging, neural architecture search, and vision-language models. He actively engages in international research collaborations and contributes to academic publishing through editorial and peer-review roles.

He serves as an Associate Editor for Frontiers in Digital Health and has received multiple recognitions including an Excellent Foreign Scholarship at SIIT and a Best Research Paper Award.
