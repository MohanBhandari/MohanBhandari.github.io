Mohan Bhandari

Mohan Bhandari received a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and a Master's degree in Computer Engineering from Pokhara University. He is currently a PhD Scholar specializing in Medical Image Processing at Sirindhorn International Institute of Technology (SIIT), Thammasat University, Thailand. His research field encompasses Explainable AI in medical imaging, neural architecture search, and vision-language models. He actively engages with a worldwide network of researchers, collaborations that significantly complement his own work. He is an associate editor for Frontiers in Digital Health and serves as a peer reviewer for numerous international journals and conferences. He has received multiple awards, including an EFS Scholarship in SIIT and a best research paper award.
